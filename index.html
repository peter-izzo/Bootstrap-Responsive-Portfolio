Nice to meet you, come get to know me a little bit...

Hi! My name is Pete. I like talking to computers and making them do cool things. I love creating new things and all things dealing with technology. Professionally, I am a web developer and programmer.

In my spare time you can find me hanging with my family, playing some video games or enjoying a nice IPA (or some combination of all three). While, I consider myself more of a homebody, I love to go snowboarding and mountain biking when time permits (and snow permits for the former).

I'm sure there is more than just that small blurb but nobody wants a full disertation on someone they just met's life so click around and check out some of the projects I've been a part of and if you REALLY do want to know more, feel free to contact me on the contact page!
